Sky Dormitory project initiated in August of 2012 by Apex Construction Company is located in Cyprus International University's southern campus and has 2000 rooms. The Sky Dormitory company's motto is to offer magnificent design and excellent comfort in rental dormitory service for the first time.

Along with being the oldest rental dormitory in Northern Cyprus it offers extraordinary services such as the largest gym of all the dorms with a 300 square meter area, a steam room, a sauna, a play area, and a cinema room.

With all its facilities Sky Dormitory Dorm combines the comfort of home with the luxury of a 5 star hotel The facilities we offer to facilitate success for our clients include a library, study room, printers and photocopy machines,wireless internet at a speed of 1000Mbps, hot water at any and all times of the day,taxi working 24/7 and an electricity generator to prevent any power cuts. To provide optimal comfort all rooms are have their own bathrooms for personal use by the occupants, rooms are furnished with the highest quality furniture, large mirrors, a kettle, burners, walls are sound proofed, and windows are double glazed. For the safety of the residents there are cameras and fire alarms installed all around and in the dorm, there are also security and reception personnel ready to provide assistance at any and all times of day.
